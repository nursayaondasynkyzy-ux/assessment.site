Speaking Assessment Score Sheet

Scoring Procedure

Each student receives a score from 0–10 in four categories:
  • Fluency and Coherence
  • Lexical Resource
  • Grammatical Range
  • Interactional Effectiveness
Final Score Formula:
(Fluency + Lexical Resource + Grammar + Interaction) ÷ 4
